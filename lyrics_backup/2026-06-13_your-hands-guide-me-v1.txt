[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The shadows fade when you pull me close
A quiet fire that nobody knows
I trace the lines upon your skin
And let this sweet slow burn begin

[Verse 1]
The midnight hour is settling in
Just breathing the air that you are in
Your fingertips upon my chest
Are putting my racing thoughts to rest

[Pre-Chorus]
Every single touch is a gentle spark
Leading me safely through the dark
I close my eyes and I just let go
Lost in the rhythm, nice and slow

[Chorus]
Your hands guide me through the night
Painting the canvas in softest light
Whenever I wander, whenever I fall
Your hands guide me through it all

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, your hands guide me through it all
[Echo] (Guide me through it all)

[Verse 2]
The embers glow in the fireplace
I read the love written on your face
I pull you closer to feel the heat
And wrap my soul in your heartbeat

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Every single sigh is a gentle spark
Leading me safely through the dark
I close my eyes and I just let go
Lost in the rhythm, nice and slow

[Chorus]
Your hands guide me through the night

Your hands guide me through it all

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, your hands guide me through it all
[Echo] (Guide me through it all)

[Bridge]
The way you touch me stops the time
Our bodies moving in perfect rhyme
With every whisper, with every sigh
You bring the fire into my sky
There is no place I would rather be
Saeed is lost in your gravity

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (Just keep your hands upon my skin)
[Spoken] (And let this beautiful dream begin)

[Final Chorus]
Your hands guide me through the night
Painting the canvas in softest light
Whenever I wander, whenever I fall
Your hands guide me through it all
